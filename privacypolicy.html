Privacy Policy

Parking Space Listing Data

a. Parking space address:

We use this to show drivers where your space is.

b. Space description:

This is a public-facing description you provide when adding your space which helps drivers decide if a space is suitable.

c. Photos of your space:

These public-facing photos help drivers decide if space is suitable and help them access it on arrival.

d. Facilities:

These are public-facing space attributes, which you can optionally provide to help drivers decide if a space is suitable for them.

e. Access instructions:

If you provide these when adding your space, they are used to help drivers access your space. They are not shown to the drivers until they paid to park in your space. Once the parking session has finished, they are hidden from the driver.

f. PIN code for accessing space:

If you provide a pin code (e.g. for a gate), it is given to drivers to help them access your space.
